How the Price is Calculated (Short Method)

Summary of formulas used by the calculator (NET – TVA Exempt). Night security is priced the same as day security. Overtime is only +25%.

Hourly Rates

  • Net hourly (info) = SMIG × (1 − employee deductions)
  • Employer cost = SMIG × (1 + employer patronal %)
  • Chargeable hourly = Employer cost × (1 + replacement %) (if enabled)
  • OT hourly (+25%) = Chargeable hourly × 1.25

Monthly Costs

  • Security Team = (Standard hours × Chargeable) + (OT hours × OT hourly) (OT only above 48h/week)
  • Cleaning Team = Cleaning agents × monthly hours/agent × Chargeable hourly
  • Transport = buses × trips/day × cost/trip × days/month
  • OPEX Total / Month = Security + Cleaning + Consumables + Transport + Other fixed
