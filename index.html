Model Notes

  • Employee CNSS/AMO rates are shown as deductions (paid by staff).
  • Employer (patronal) contributions are added to gross wage to compute employer cost.
  • Replacement coefficient applies to employer cost for continuity.
  • PPE & equipment are treated as one-time CAPEX (purchased once).
  • Tax display: NET (TVA Exempt – Tangier Free Zone), subject to eligibility/conditions.
